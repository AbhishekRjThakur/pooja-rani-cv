About Me

The designer behind the seams.

I am Pooja Rani, a fashion designer from Punjab, India, shaped by the rhythm of Indian textiles and the precision of European silhouettes. I design garments that balance comfort, culture and a quiet sense of drama.

My journey into fashion began with a fascination for how fabric moves around the body. From early sketches in school notebooks to structured design training, I have always treated garments as moving sculptures that tell stories for the wearer.

Working with Punjab Fashion House refined my understanding of garment construction, client fittings and the discipline behind every seam. At Pol Green in Croatia, I experienced European design sensibilities and sustainable material choices, blending these influences with my Indian roots to create a personal, cross-cultural design language.

Today, I am focused on silhouettes that feel considered from every angle, patterns that honour the body‚Äôs movement, and finishes that give each piece a couture-like attention to detail. Whether it is backstage at a show or inside a quiet studio, I stay committed to listening, learning and designing garments that truly serve the people who wear them.
